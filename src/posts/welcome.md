Hello, and welcome to my corner of the internet.

This blog is a space for writing about the things that occupy my mind—professionally and personally. On the professional side, that means **AI policy**, **data science**, and **analysis**. On the personal side, expect thoughts on **food**, **drink**, **cooking**, and **music**.

## why another blog?

Because writing helps me think. And thinking in public invites conversation.

## what to expect

Posts will vary in length and formality. Some might be polished essays; others, rough sketches of ideas still forming. The common thread is curiosity.

Thanks for reading.
